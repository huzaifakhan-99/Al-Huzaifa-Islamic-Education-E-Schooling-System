Who we are

A digital madrasah, run like a school.

Al Huzaifa Islamic Education & E-Schooling System was founded to solve one problem: families wanted authentic, structured Qur’anic and Islamic education for their children, but couldn’t always find it nearby. We bring the madrasah — and the classroom — to your screen, taught by ijazah-holding teachers with proper lesson plans, progress reports, and real accountability.

  • Sanad-connected teachers. Every Qur’an tutor holds a verified ijazah in Tajweed or Qira’ah.
  • One student, one teacher. Live one-to-one sessions, not pre-recorded videos.
  • Real report cards. Monthly progress reports for both Qur’an and school subjects.
  • Built for every schedule. Morning, evening and weekend slots across time zones.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

What do I need for online classes?

A smartphone, tablet or computer with a stable internet connection. We teach over a simple video-call platform — no special software required.

Is the trial class really free?

Yes. Every new student receives one full free trial class with their matched teacher before any payment is made.

Can my child take more than one course?

Yes — many students combine the School Program with Hifz or Nazra. We build a combined timetable so classes never clash.

How are teachers verified?

Every Qur’an teacher holds a recognized ijazah and is tested on Tajweed and recitation before teaching. School teachers are verified for relevant qualifications and experience.

What if the schedule doesn’t suit us after the trial?

There is no obligation. You can request a different teacher, a different time slot, or simply not continue — the trial class is commitment-free.
